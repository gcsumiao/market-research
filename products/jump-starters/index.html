Product Overview

Innova Portable Compact Inflator / Jump Starter

Innova is developing a 2-in-1 portable jump starter and tire inflator combo in two form factors: a compact 3.5” pocket unit and a full-size A3 model rated at 3000A / 150PSI. Both feature digital PSI displays, jump starter clamps, USB charging, and emergency SOS lights — wrapped in Innova’s signature red and black industrial design.

Key differentiator: Innova is the only brand in this space with deep automotive diagnostics expertise (OBD-II scanners, code readers). The potential for battery health diagnostics, alternator testing, or OBD integration built into a jump starter would be a genuine first-to-market feature no competitor offers.

NOCO’s Combo Vulnerability

Despite dominating the overall jump starter market (32.6% share, $11.3M/mo), NOCO generates only $207K/month from combo products — just 1.8% of its revenue comes from the fastest-growing segment. Its sole combo entry (Boost+Air AX65, $300) has the lowest rating among top combos at 4.1★. Meanwhile, GOOLOO earns 62% of its revenue ($1.8M/mo) from combos, and WOLFBOX earns 66% ($1.6M/mo) from combos. This signals that the combo segment is being won by newer, more agile brands — exactly the opportunity for Innova.

An Innova combo at $100–$130 entering the GOOLOO A3 / WOLFBOX sweet spot has a clear path to $500K+/month in Year 1 on Amazon alone.
