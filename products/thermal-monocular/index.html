Decision lens

Why 384 Pro leads the launch
  • 256 Entry rides proven Amazon velocity: TOPDON TS004 (~915 u/mo) and GOYOJO 256 (~317 u/mo) at $370–$407 show real paying demand at this spec tier.9
  • 384 Pro hits a clean whitespace: RIX Pocket K3 and TS004 Pro both at $899 — a $599–$699 50 Hz / 25 mm 384 SKU has no current direct competitor on Amazon.
  • 640 Flagship gives Innova a future halo spec to anchor brand trust in retail and DTC, but only after preorder or retail-pilot validation.
  • 384 Pro keeps the launch forecast clean: it carries the strongest margin and whitespace story while 256 Entry and 640 Flagship remain optional expansion levers.
Why it's hard
  • 256 Entry at 25 Hz is below TOPDON TS004's 50 Hz at a similar price — hunting buyers will notice in reviews.
  • 384 Pro spec sheet has internal contradictions (min-focus 3 m vs 1.5 m; WiFi listed but TBD) that must be confirmed before launch.
  • 640 Flagship at $1,499–$1,999 sits beside RIX Titan-T6 ($2,399) and AGM Sidewinder ($1,595) — thin Amazon velocity, retail-validated buyer.
  • No retail shelf moat at launch. Defensibility rests on US-brand trust, warranty terms, and disciplined feature execution.

Where offline shelf exists

Outdoor / hunting retail is the only real brick-and-mortar category. Bass Pro / Cabela's stock Pulsar, FLIR, ATN, AGM, Armasight across $250–$5,000. Walmart carries TOPDON & HEXEUM online, almost nothing in-aisle.8

Where it doesn't

Auto parts and tool retail are structurally absent. Harbor Freight sells a $199 spot IR camera, not thermal optics. AutoZone has zero handheld NV. O'Reilly stocks the Innova 3360 thermal imaging camera — adjacent but not a monocular.625

Retail context for Stage-3 only: Outdoor/hunting retail (Bass Pro / Cabela's / Sportsman's) remains the only real brick-and-mortar shelf for thermal monoculars. Auto retail and tool retail are structurally absent at launch. Ecommerce-first strategy means none of this gates the launch — it only informs where a Year-2 retail-tier variant could go once Amazon BSR has been earned.
